Layers

Intro

This guide will help you set up layers and begin drawing.

Creating a Layer

To create a layer, simply click the "Add Layer" button on the left side of the screen. In future versions you will have to determine the type of layer you want to add

Drawing On The Layer

To draw on the layer, simply click in the middle of the screen. To change tools, click the different tool buttons on the top of the screen.

Types of Layers

(This section mostly focuses on future content as of writing)

Bitmap Layers

The essential layers for drawing. These contain a "Bitmap" also known as a Raster image. They can be drawn on with all the tools on the top panel.

Bone Layers

Bone layers are used exclusively for animation rigs. Each bone layer contains a "Bone" which can be resized and moved around freely.

GIF Layers

GIF layers are used as a simpler way of animating. They contain a GIF file and can not be drawn on. The GIF's framerate is tied to the animation's

Audio Layers

Audio layers are used to play audio.
